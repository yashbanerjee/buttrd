OUR STORY

Buttrd didn't start with a menu. It started in a home kitchen, with the two of us and one obsession: getting a sauce just right.

It was butter chicken—but not the way we knew it. We kept going back to it. Tweaking, adjusting, tasting. Too rich. Too flat. Too tangy. Too safe. And then it clicked—balancing richness with a slight sweetness, adding a gentle tang—still comforting, still familiar, but with a character of its own.

A sauce that didn't just sit on the food, but carried it. That first sauce became our foundation. Not just a recipe, but a way of thinking.

We grew up on the classics—the dishes you crave after a long day, the ones you go back to without thinking. They are familiar and reliable, but also predictable. Somewhere along the way, we realized there was so much more they could be. What if it worked beyond the obvious? In a sandwich. With paneer. Over pasta? What if comfort food didn't have to stay in its lane—not by reinventing food completely, but by reimagining what already works.

One sauce turned into a concept. A concept turned into a menu. And that menu became Buttrd. Today, every dish we serve follows that same idea: take something people already love, and give them a reason to fall in love with it all over again.

What we believe

  • Flavour comes first — always
  • A great sauce can change everything
  • Simple food doesn't have to be boring

Because comfort food shouldn't be boring. And at Buttrd, it never is.
